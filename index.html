Our Sourcing Advantage

How We Operate
Direct Sourcing from Factories

We directly source our natural stones from a network of trusted partner factories. This ensures that our clients receive products directly from the manufacturing source, guaranteeing authenticity and quality.

Strategic
Raw Material Supply

Tons India stands apart due to our strategic supply of raw materials to our partnered factories. This close relationship allows us to have an in-depth understanding of their stocks and pricing structures.

Procurement
Bulk Procurement Benefits

By providing raw materials at a substantial scale, we secure favorable pricing from our partner factories. This enables us to pass on these cost benefits to our clients, ensuring competitive prices without compromising on quality.

No Overheads
Lower Prices

We operate without the burdensome overhead costs associated with maintaining extensive stock. Instead, we utilize our expertise to handpick select marble blocks for ourselves when procuring in bulk for our factories. This not only ensures a continuous supply but also allows us to offer our clients lower prices.
